# touchscreen-touchpad
A computer trackpad made out of an Arduino Leonardo for native USB capability, a 3D printed chassis, and a touchscreen based on the FT6336U touch digitizer and ST7796S display driver. Features implemented include:
Single finger dragging to move the mouse cursor
Single finger tap to left click
Two finger tap to right click
Two finger vertical swipe to scroll
Two finger horizontal swipe to horizontally scroll (Holds Shift and scrolls)
Two finger pinch to zoom (Holds Ctrl and scrolls)
